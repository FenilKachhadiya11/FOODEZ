About Foodez

The journey of Food is called FOODEZ

Kudoz began with the idea of creating an innovatively designed space for youngsters
to hang around and relish our ice cream, beverages, and snacks. The ambience of Kudoz has found resonance with our discerning customers who love to spend time at our store and explore our scrumptious range. Our stores are expanding by leaps and bounds in Gujarat and we are open to Franchise opportunities.
